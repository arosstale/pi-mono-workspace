### 3. Friction Points (Scar Tissue) ü©π
**File:** `FRICTION_POINTS.md`

**What It Tracks:**

- Value misalignment (optimized for wrong priority)

**The Win:** "Scar tissue" ‚Äî healed wounds show us what to avoid.
