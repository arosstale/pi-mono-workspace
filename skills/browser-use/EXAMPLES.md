## Tips

1. **Be Specific**: "Click the 'Sign In' button in the top right" vs "Click sign in"
2. **Break Down Tasks**: Multi-step workflows work better when described step by step
3. **Provide Context**: Mention page state when relevant
4. **Use Natural Language**: Describe what you want, not how to do it
